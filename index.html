About

We are a research group at Gdansk University of Technology working at the intersection of AI, distributed systems, privacy, and verifiable infrastructure.

Our focus is on systems that must operate under real constraints: sensitive data, adversarial environments, organizational boundaries, and deployment pressure. We care about AI that is not only capable, but measurable, safe, and accountable.

Location Gdansk, Poland
Department Computer Architecture, FETI
Group lead Prof. Julian Szymanski
Focus AI · Privacy · Distributed Systems

Research Areas

What we work on

01
Large Language Models & Applied AI

Evaluation, prototyping, and deployment of LLM-based systems for knowledge work, classification, decision support, and automation.

02
Trustworthy AI Systems

Reliability, robustness, accountability, and safety of AI systems used in sensitive organizational workflows.

03
Privacy-Preserving & Verifiable Computation

Systems that enable useful computation while reducing unnecessary data exposure and making critical claims verifiable.

04
Blockchain & Decentralized Infrastructure

Protocols and architectures for auditability, provenance, distributed trust, and resilient digital public infrastructure.
